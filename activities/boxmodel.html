What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags start on a new line and take up the full length available by default while inline tags do not start on a new line and take up only the necessary space needed

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Padding is the space between the content and the border, border is the visibal edge around the padding and content and margin is the space outside the border
